December Motivational Video

I made this video as a way to express how I personally see December and the idea of endings in general. For many people, December feels like the final stop — the end of the year, the closing of unfinished goals, and sometimes even a reminder of time passing too quickly. I wanted to capture that feeling at first, which is why the video opens in a dimly lit room with the words “December is the end” written on the wall. The darkness reflects the heaviness that comes with the thought of closure. But I didn’t want to leave the message there. For me, December has always been more than an ending; it’s also the doorway to something new. That’s why I used the act of switching on the lamp as a turning point. When the warm light fills the room, the text changes to “December is just another chapter… not the end of your story.” This transformation represents a shift in mindset — from seeing life as a series of endings to seeing it as a continuous story with chapters that build on one another. The computer monitor, the desk, and the lamp are all simple, everyday objects, but that was intentional. I wanted to show that meaning doesn’t always come from grand things — even a small change, like turning on a lamp, can completely transform the mood of a space. Similarly, a change in perspective can completely transform how we see our own lives. The choice of warm yellow and green text adds to that feeling of renewal and positivity, almost like a handwritten reminder you might leave for yourself on a sticky note. For me, this video isn’t just about December; it’s about life in general. We often mistake an ending for a full stop, when really it’s just a comma in our story. By making this piece, I wanted to remind myself — and anyone watching — that every end is just a new beginning waiting to unfold. It’s about hope, resilience, and the belief that our stories don’t stop; they keep going, chapter by chapter.
